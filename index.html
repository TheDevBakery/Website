About

The Dev Bakery is the latest incarnation of the mainly .net focused developer user group that has previously been called NxtGen Manchester and DareDevs. We are now based in the centre of Altrincham, and we're easy accessible by road, rail and air!

We aim to meet on the third wednesday of every month from 7-9PM, the evenings will consist of a primary speaker, occasionally a secondary speaker, a break in the middle for pizza, and a trip to the pub afterwards!

By Road:
We are located right in the centre of Altrincham, there are plenty of carparks around, and some on street free parking, but it's a bit of a luck of the draw how busy they are. We'd recommend you leave at least 20minutes to find parking the first time you come along, and take a look at Parkopedia to see some of the options. The ice rink car park is free after 6pm

By Rail:
Altrincham tram/train station is 2 minutes walk from the office.

By Air:
We are only a short taxi ride away from Manchester Airport ;)
